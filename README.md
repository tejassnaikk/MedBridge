# 💊 MedBridge — Closing the Medication Access Gap

> Connecting surrendered, unexpired medications with patients who can't afford them.


---

## The Problem

Every year, Americans discard **hundreds of millions of dollars** worth of unexpired, sealed prescription medications — after stopping a treatment, following a loved one's passing, or switching prescriptions. At the same time, millions of low-income patients and free clinics face impossible choices between medication and basic necessities.

**There is no safe, legal, easy way to connect these two groups.**

Existing drug take-back programs focus entirely on safe disposal. They destroy medications that could still help someone. MedBridge adds a **matching layer** on top of that infrastructure — so surrendered drugs reach people who need them, not the incinerator.

---

## What We Built

MedBridge is a web platform that enables:

- 🏥 **Donors** (clinics, assisted living facilities, pharmacies, take-back sites) to log surrendered, unexpired, sealed medications
- 🔍 **Recipients** (low-income patients, free clinics, community health workers) to search available inventory and request specific medications
- 🤝 **Coordinators** to verify, approve, and facilitate safe transfers within existing legal frameworks

We're not building new infrastructure. We're building the **matching layer** that existing take-back programs are missing.

---

## Features

### For Donor Organizations
- Simple intake form to log surrendered medications (drug name, dosage, quantity, expiration date, lot number)
- Dashboard to manage current inventory and pending requests
- Automatic flagging of controlled substances (excluded from matching per DEA guidelines)
- Transfer documentation generation for compliance

### For Recipient Organizations & Patients
- Search available medications by drug name, dosage, or condition
- Request specific medications with basic needs verification
- Geolocation-based matching to minimize shipping/transfer distance
- Status notifications for request approvals

### For Platform Coordinators
- Verification queue for both donors and recipients
- Audit logs for all transfers
- Expiration date monitoring and automatic de-listing
- Analytics dashboard (medications redirected, estimated dollar value, patients served)
